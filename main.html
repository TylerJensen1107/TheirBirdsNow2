About

Their Birds Now is an indie-folk project, with the debut EP Malaron" written and recorded in the summer of 2015 by Peyton Levin. The songs draw inspiration from bands such as Bon Iver and Sufjan Stevens, while seeking to include elements of classical theory. Peyton's vocal style ranges from falsetto in the opening track to a lower register in Tell Me Something, and is often backed by harmonies and vocal doubling. The vocals are set to acoustic guitar, electric pianos, and the occasional shaker.

Live, the group plays with a full arrangement- a female and male vocalist, drums, electric guitar, bass, and keyboard
